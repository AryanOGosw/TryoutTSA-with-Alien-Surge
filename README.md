**Essay:**

Space Invaders is one of the most iconic games of the 80s, with almost 1 billion plays in its first year, grossing over $670 million in Japan alone, and with a daily revenue being $114 million in its first year. Space Invaders is one of the first video games to have such widespread popularity and was the main reason for the success in the gaming industry. Our Video Game Design team chose this influential game so that we could incorporate our style of gaming (platformer) into a video game, including aspects of this iconic retro game. We kept the original theme of combating aliens, but added modern style gameplay, which is like what you would see in many other platformers. By incorporating these styles and having many past experiences with Space Invaders, we were able to make a simple yet interactive and enjoyable game.
